Who Am I?

Hi and welcome to my site! My name is Amy and I am a software engineer. I focus primarily on web development but have also worked on mobile apps. I am passionate about learning new technologies, encouraging women and girls to fall in love with tech, and traveling the world.

       

  Experience

Software Engineer @ Cigna

I am currently the tech lead and lead developer for a social fitness competition web application. This site allows participants to track active minutes manually or using wearable devices and compete against co-workers as individuals or as part of a team. The site currently has over 30,000 users and is used in 16 countries around the world. My role involves leading a team of developers, helping to facilitate architectural decisions, interfacing with business partners, and writing lots of code.

In addition to my project work, I am heavily involved in the technical community at Cigna. I mentor younger developers on my team and across the company, am involved in the planning committee for our internal technical conference, and co-lead a group devoted to sharing information regarding single page web applications.
